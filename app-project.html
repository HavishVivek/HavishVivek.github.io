My Productivity App

Track tasks, earn tokens, and unlock celebrations. A fun way to stay productive!

About the App

This app helps kids and parents stay engaged with tasks through a token-based reward system. Kids complete tasks to earn tokens, and parents can schedule celebrations when goals are achieved. Built with Flutter + Firebase.

App Demo Video

What platforms does the app support?

The app is available on Android and iOS.

Do tokens reset every day?

No, tokens are cumulative until goals are met.

Can parents customize celebrations?

Yes, parents can add, edit, and schedule custom celebrations.
